Coimbatore - The Textile City

About Coimbatore

Coimbatore, located in the state of Tamil Nadu, is renowned as the "Manchester of South India" for its extensive textile industry. The city is a major hub for textiles, engineering, and educational institutions.

Known for its pleasant climate and scenic surroundings, Coimbatore is a perfect blend of tradition and modernity. The city also serves as a gateway to various tourist destinations in the region.

Discover Coimbatore

Fun Facts About Coimbatore

1. Coimbatore is often referred to as the "Pump City" due to its extensive pump manufacturing industry.

2. The city is home to one of the largest textile manufacturing sectors in India, earning it the nickname "Textile City".

3. Coimbatore has a thriving educational scene with numerous reputed institutions, including the renowned PSG College of Technology.

4. The city's pleasant weather and proximity to hill stations make it a popular destination for tourists and nature enthusiasts.
