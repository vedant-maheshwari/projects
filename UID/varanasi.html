Varanasi - The Spiritual Heart of India

About Varanasi

Varanasi, also known as Kashi, is one of the oldest living cities in the world. Situated on the banks of the sacred Ganges River, it is a major center for spiritual and religious activities in India.

The city is famous for its ghats, temples, and vibrant cultural scene. Pilgrims and tourists from all over the world visit Varanasi to experience its spiritual essence and witness the evening Ganga Aarti.

Discover Varanasi

Fun Facts About Varanasi

1. Varanasi is considered the spiritual capital of India, with thousands of temples and ghats along the Ganges River.

2. The city is believed to be over 3,000 years old, making it one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world.

3. The Ganga Aarti, a devotional ritual on the ghats, attracts numerous visitors every evening for its mesmerizing spectacle.

4. Varanasi is known for its classical music, silk weaving, and renowned educational institutions like Banaras Hindu University.
