About Gwalior

Gwalior, a city with a rich historical background, is located in the heart of India. Known for its majestic palaces, ancient temples, and vibrant culture, Gwalior offers a unique blend of history and modernity.

The city's history is reflected in its impressive forts and palaces, which have stood the test of time. Gwalior is also a hub of classical music, being the birthplace of Tansen, one of India's greatest musicians.

Discover Gwalior

Fun Facts About Gwalior

1. Gwalior Fort is one of the most impenetrable forts in India, often referred to as the "Gibraltar of India".

2. The city is known for its annual Tansen Music Festival, which celebrates Indian classical music.

3. Gwalior is home to the second oldest reference library in Asia, the Scindia Oriental Research Institute.

4. The city's Jai Vilas Palace boasts one of the world's largest chandeliers, weighing several tons.
