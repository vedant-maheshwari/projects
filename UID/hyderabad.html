Hyderabad - The City of Pearls

About Hyderabad

Hyderabad, the capital city of Telangana, is known as the "City of Pearls" due to its historic role in the pearl and diamond trading industry. It is a city that beautifully blends its rich history with modernity.

The city is famous for its Mughal architecture, bustling bazaars, and vibrant culinary scene. Hyderabad is also a major center for the technology industry, often referred to as "Cyberabad".

Discover Hyderabad

Fun Facts About Hyderabad

1. Hyderabad is home to the famous Charminar, a 16th-century mosque that stands as an iconic symbol of the city.

2. The city is renowned for its Hyderabadi biryani, a rich and aromatic rice dish that has a global following.

3. Hyderabad's Hussain Sagar Lake, built in 1563, is one of the largest man-made lakes in India and is known for its large Buddha statue.

4. The Ramoji Film City in Hyderabad is the world's largest film studio complex, attracting filmmakers from around the globe.
