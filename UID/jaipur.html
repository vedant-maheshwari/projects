Jaipur - The Pink City

About Jaipur

Jaipur, the capital of Rajasthan, is known as the "Pink City" for its distinctively colored buildings. The city is a major tourist destination in India, famed for its majestic palaces, forts, and vibrant culture.

Jaipur forms part of the Golden Triangle tourist circuit along with Delhi and Agra. It is a gateway to other tourist attractions in Rajasthan and showcases a fascinating blend of tradition and modernity.

Discover Jaipur

Fun Facts About Jaipur

1. Jaipur was founded in 1727 by Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II, and is known for its well-planned streets and architecture.

2. The city is famous for its pink-colored buildings, which were painted to welcome the Prince of Wales in 1876.

3. Jaipur is part of the UNESCO World Heritage Sites, recognized for its rich cultural and architectural heritage.

4. The city is a hub for traditional arts and crafts, including jewelry, textiles, and handicrafts.

Explore Jaipur

Historic Forts

Explore the majestic forts of Jaipur, including Amber Fort, Nahargarh Fort, and Jaigarh Fort, offering stunning views and historical insights.

Royal Palaces

Visit the royal palaces of Jaipur, such as the City Palace and Hawa Mahal, known for their architectural grandeur and historical significance.

Cultural Heritage

Immerse yourself in Jaipur's cultural heritage, experiencing traditional music, dance, and art forms that are integral to the city's identity.

Local Cuisine

Savor the local cuisine of Jaipur, known for its rich flavors and traditional dishes such as dal bati churma, ghewar, and kachori.
