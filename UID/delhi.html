Delhi - Explore the Capital Wonders

About Delhi

Delhi, officially known as the National Capital Territory of Delhi (NCT), is a bustling metropolis located in northern India. Renowned for its rich history, vibrant culture, and diverse population, Delhi offers a unique blend of tradition and modernity, making it a must-visit destination for travelers.

The city boasts a history that dates back millennia, with landmarks and monuments that stand as a testament to its glorious past. From ancient forts to modern skyscrapers, Delhi has something to offer for every type of explorer.

Discover Delhi

Fun Facts About Delhi

1. Delhi is home to the tallest minaret in India, Qutub Minar, which stands at 73 meters tall.

2. The city's metro system is one of the largest and busiest in the world, covering over 300 kilometers.

3. Delhi is known as the "City of Rallies" due to its frequent political and social gatherings.

4. The city's skyline is a mix of historic monuments and modern buildings, creating a unique architectural landscape.
